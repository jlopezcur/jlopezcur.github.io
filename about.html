Hi! My name is Javier and I'm a web developer.

I would like to tell you something about me. I started to develop professional software around the year 2000, the Internet at that time was still precarious and websites were made with HTML/CSS and little or no javascript, the king was Flash. In all this years a lot of changes comes and in our days nobody think in the web in the same way. Now we have a lot of technologies for every minimal part of the software development. I love this changes and the adaptation I had to do for being part of all of this, It was a continously learning process that (I think) each professional of this sector must to accept, to love and to be passionated for this.

Alone we are smart but together we are brilliant. We can use the collective wisdom to do great things when we are connected.
