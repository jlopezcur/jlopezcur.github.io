Hi! My name is Javier and I'm a web developer.

I would like to tell you something about me. I started to develop professional software around the year 2000, the Internet at that time was still precarious and websites were made with HTML/CSS and little or no javascript, the king was Flash. In all these years a lot of changes have happened and in our days nobody sees the web in the same way. Now we have a lot of technologies for every minimal part in software development. I love these changes and the adaptation I had to do to become part of all this. It was a constant learning process that (in my opinion) each professional within this sector must accept, love and be passionate about.

Alone we are smart but together we are brilliant. We can use the collective wisdom to do great things when we are connected.

Steven W. Anderson

In the skills page I tried to show all the technologies that have crossed my path so you can have a better picture of my expertise.

As you can see the site is using some look & feel from known terminal software (like dselect).

The title of this portfolio was inspired by my favourite TV show "Supernatural".
